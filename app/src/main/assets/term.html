MEASUREMENTS

Every fabric has a unique weight, feel and fall- hence measurements may appear to be different. Please be prepared for slight variances in fit based on fabrics.

All measurements provided are final product measurements and garments will be produced within a 0.5” tolerance of those measurements.

You can store your measurements to your account for future reference. However, your last used measurements will automatically be saved and will override any previously saved measurements.

ALTERATIONS

  • Most garments cannot be altered to a lower size more than 2" variance i.e.; a size of 32" can at a maximum be reduced to 30" and not beyond.
  • We do alterations on almost anything that is reasonably possible but like always there are certain exceptions such as crochets, collars, below the hip for Denims and increasing the lengths and widths.
  • For the well-being of your garments we do not accept clothes that were dyed.
  • Kindly take your time to send relevant garments only as samples.
  • Snap buttons cannot be replaced and if at all you lose a button it is only possible to replace all the buttons with new ones as find a match to the existing ones is next to impossible.
  • For Seam Fixing we will try to match with the same colour in 99% of the cases and in the remaining cases we shall match with the closest dark shade.

LOYALTY POINTS

Each time you buy a product, you will receive loyalty points based on the value of the purchased shirts. Each point is worth One Rupee. Use these points on subsequent orders or save up enough points to earn free shirts. Points earned through shopping online, through our Stores or Travelling Stylist, are all credited to your online profile. These points may be redeemed through any of the above channels in the future. Loyalty points are credited after your products have been shipped to you.

Loyalty points are awarded on the base price of the product, not on any additional charges incurred. Points are not awarded for purchase of gift cards or fabrics offered at clearance prices at the store.

Loyalty points are calculated on the net paid value of the product, after having used previously earned loyalty points towards the order. Loyalty points cannot be redeemed as cash.

The company reserves the right to change the terms of the loyalty program.
